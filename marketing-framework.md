# NMW Growth Framework

## Distribution

Artists, media personalities, DJs, and attendees distribute NMW by sharing:

* interviews
* performance clips
* flyers
* event links
* recap content
* live moments

Every artist appearance should create content built to spread.

---

## Amplification

NMW amplifies artists through:

* homepage features
* interviews
* live performances
* media coverage
* DJ exposure
* Instagram clips
* YouTube content

The platform should make artists feel momentum around their name.

---

## Validation

NMW creates credibility through:

* Digiwaxx DJ Call
* media personalities
* live interviews
* crowd reactions
* performances
* media pit coverage

Artists should feel:
“If I perform here, people take me seriously.”

---

## Viral Loops

### Artist Loop

Artist signs up →
shares with team/fans →
new attendees/artists join →
more performances/content →
more artists want access

### Referral Loop

Invite artists →
unlock rewards →
more artist registrations →
more audience growth

### Attendance Loop

RSVP →
Google Calendar →
weekly reminders →
recurring attendance

### Content Loop

Every Wednesday creates:

* clips
* interviews
* recaps
* reactions
* performance moments

Content fuels the next event.

---

## Positioning

NMW is not just an event.

It is:

* a discovery platform
* a rollout engine
* a media ecosystem
* a cultural hub for emerging artists

---

## UX Direction

Keep everything:

* mobile first
* fast
* social
* culturally authentic
* clean and high energy

Avoid:

* fake hype
* clutter
* unnecessary gamification
* corporate event language
