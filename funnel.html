Join The Media Team

Media isn't on the sidelines here, it's at the center of the culture. Every week, the media pit is where conversations spark, relationships are built, and moments get documented as they happen, all during a live taping and streamed production. Interviews come to life. Real reactions. You never know who might pop up.

It's about bringing back that real in-the-room energy, the kind you can feel. Building relationships. Capturing authentic stories. Connecting the culture to the world through media, streaming, and in-person coverage.

From celebrity guests to next-up talent, the room stays alive, and our media partners keep that energy moving far beyond the walls.
