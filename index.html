How It Works

The Digiwaxx DJ Call

Every week starts with the legendary Digiwaxx Zoom call featuring DJs, tastemakers, artists, and industry conversations centered around new music and what's gaining attention.

The Happy Hour Experience

Curated cocktails. Real networking. Creative energy.

Meet artists, DJs, influencers, producers, media personalities, and creatives inside an exclusive NYC environment built for conversation and collaboration.

The Next Up Experience

Selected artists perform live, debut records, create content, sit for interviews, and connect directly with DJs, media, influencers, and tastemakers.

This is where artists turn releases into real-world conversation and visibility.
