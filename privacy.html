Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

1. Who we are

New Music Wednesdays ("NMW", "we", "us") is a live performance and rollout platform presented by Digiwaxx. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and what your choices are.

2. What we collect

  • Account details you provide when you apply, RSVP, or subscribe: name, artist name, email, phone (optional), genre, location, social handles.
  • Payment information processed by Stripe. We never store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, referring source, and basic device info, to improve the experience.

3. How we use it

  • To run shows, confirm bookings, and deliver the package you purchased.
  • To send event details, alerts, and the weekly Blast newsletter (you can unsubscribe any time).
  • To distribute your music and event listings through partner networks (Digiwaxx DJ network, DICE, Bandsintown, Songkick, Eventbrite, Luma).
  • To prevent fraud and keep our community safe.

4. Sharing

We share data with the partners listed above only to deliver the services you signed up for, plus standard infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics, payments). We do not sell your personal information.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing privacy@digiwaxx.com. You can also unsubscribe from email at any time using the link in any newsletter.

6. Cookies

We use a small number of essential and analytics cookies. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

7. Updates

We may update this policy as the platform changes. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@digiwaxx.com.
