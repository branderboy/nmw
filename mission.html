[ Our Mission ]

We don't book stages.
We build rollouts.

New Music Wednesdays exists to make sure every rising artist who steps on our stage walks off with a real audience, real coverage, and infrastructure that compounds beyond the night.

New Music Wednesdays started inside Digiwaxx — the DJ network that has quietly broken records for over two decades. We saw what was missing: artists with real talent kept getting one-night spotlights and zero rollout. The clip went up. The momentum didn't last. The audience never showed up to the next show.

So we built a different kind of stage. One where the performance is just the trigger — and what happens after is the actual product. Press write-ups. Distribution. DICE relationships. DJ exposure. Promo assets you can use for months. The kind of infrastructure that turns a Wednesday night into a real career inflection point.

That's the mission: turn culture into compounding visibility for the artists who deserve it.

[ What we believe ]

Four pillars hold the whole platform up.

Amplification

Performances should produce moments — clips, interviews, photos, recap content — that spread far beyond the room they happened in.

Distribution

Every show should ship into the systems where fans actually find music: DICE, Bandsintown, Songkick, the Digiwaxx DJ network, and your own audience.

Validation

A media pit, a DJ Call, hosts who actually move culture — proof that an artist is taken seriously when they walk in the room.

Relationships

DJs, videographers, producers, label reps, legends of the culture — the room is built so connections happen naturally, not forced.
