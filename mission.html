From the founders & the hosts

We have stood in too many rooms where real talent didn't get seen. Rooms where the lights went up and the people who needed to hear it were already on their phones. Rooms where the artist gave everything and walked out with nothing, no clip, no contact, no next thing. We built this stage because we couldn't keep watching that happen.

New Music Wednesdays isn't a showcase. It's not a try-out. It's a commitment. The commitment is this: if you walk on our stage, we will not let your night disappear into the air. We will catch it on camera. We will write you up. We will push you to the people who matter, DJs, media, the audience that becomes your audience.

We know what it costs you to keep showing up. The DM that didn't get answered. The flyer that didn't move tickets. The session that didn't lead anywhere. The voice that says maybe this isn't for me. We hear that voice too. And we are telling you, on the record:

"It's for you. We made the room for you."

Every Wednesday at The Penthouse, the lights come up at 7. The DJ Call has been running since 3. The hosts, Anna, CL, Chrys, are warming up the crowd that came specifically to find someone new. The cameras are on. The press is in the pit. There is a real audience in front of you, the kind that came because they trust this room to show them what's next.

That's the room you walk into. That's what we're protecting. That's what this whole thing is for.

We see you. We're ready when you are.

CL Llewellyn

[ Our Promise ]

When you step on this stage,
we owe you something.

1

We owe you an active community.

Not your friends. Not the bartender. Not even a quiet, polite audience. People who came because they trust this room to show them what's next, then move for the artists they discover. They Shazam, they pre-save, they share the clip, they carry your name to the next room. A community, not a crowd.

2

We owe you the moment in HD.

The clip. The photo. The interview. Assets you can carry into next week, next month, next year, the kind of receipts that turn a Wednesday into a year of momentum.

3

We owe you the rooms after this one.

Your show ships into DICE, Bandsintown, Songkick, and the New Music Wednesday DJ network. Your performance becomes searchable. People who weren't there get to find you on purpose.

4

We owe you the truth.

We will tell you what worked. We will tell you what didn't. We are not in the business of selling anybody dreams. The room is real, the press is real, the relationships are real, and we will only sell you what we can deliver.
