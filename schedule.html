Three blocks. One Wednesday.

Each week kicks off with the legendary Digiwaxx DJ Call, where tastemakers and DJs nationwide set the tone for what's next. On the ground, New Media Wednesdays transforms the space into a creative hub. Artists step into the spotlight through The Next Up Experience, The Booth, and Barz at the Bar. Every Wednesday is designed to discover new music, amplify emerging voices, and spark collaborations.

Every Wednesday produces signal.

The DJ Call decides what gets played. The Media Pit decides what gets seen. Live performances decide what gets remembered. Every Wednesday is designed to discover new music, amplify emerging voices, and spark collaborations that ripple through the culture — from the Digiwaxx DJ network to the press pit to the room itself.

Discover

New records find their first audience — on the podcast, in the press pit, and on stage.

Amplify

Collaborate
